Frequently asked questions

Got a question about your wellness journey

  • What is wellness coaching, and how can it benefit me?

    Wellness coaching recognizes the interconnectedness of various aspects of a person's life and prioritizes individualized care and self-compassion. It aims to empower individuals to take an active role in their own health and well-being by providing them with the confidence, knowledge, and skills necessary for making positive lifestyle changes. The coaching process may include goal setting, action planning, and ongoing support and accountability.

  • How do I schedule a wellness consultation?

    Scheduling a consultation is easy. Simply Click here to book your free consultation online. We'll reach out to you to confirm the date and time.

  • What can I expect during a consultation?

    During your consultation, we'll discuss your goals, challenges and preferences. We'll create a personalised plan to address your unique needs and set you on the path to well-being.

  • Are your wellness services offered online or in-person?

    We offer both in-person and online wellness sessions, providing flexibility to accommodate your preferences and schedule.
